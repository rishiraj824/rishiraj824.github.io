# The Journey Through Tech Interviews

In the ever-evolving landscape of technology, the interview process has become a rite of passage for every developer. Today, I want to share my personal journey through numerous technical interviews and the valuable lessons I've learned along the way.

## The Initial Screening Phase

My journey began with the recruiter screening phase, where I encountered my first set of challenges:

- **Replit**: Didn't get an opportunity
- **StubHub**: Didn't get an opportunity
- **HarveyAI**: Didn't get an opportunity

These early rejections taught me the importance of having a strong initial pitch and understanding what companies are looking for in their screening process.

## The Technical Interview Marathon

The technical screening phase was where I faced the most intense challenges. Here's a list of companies where I participated in technical interviews:

- Hooglee
- Airtable
- Microsoft AI
- Imbue
- Mixpanel
- Eventual
- Roblox
- Open AI
- Factory AI
- Heygen
- Perplexity AI

Each of these interviews was a learning experience in itself. While I didn't succeed in these rounds, they provided invaluable insights into different interview styles, problem-solving approaches, and company expectations.

## Current Opportunities

Despite the challenges, I'm excited to share that I'm moving forward with several promising opportunities:

- Doordash
- ScaleAI
- Figma

## Lessons Learned

1. **Preparation is Key**: Each interview taught me something new about how to prepare better for the next one.

2. **Resilience Matters**: Facing multiple rejections can be disheartening, but it's crucial to maintain perspective and keep moving forward.

3. **Learning Never Stops**: Every interview, whether successful or not, is an opportunity to learn and grow.

4. **Company Culture**: Through these interviews, I've gained insights into different company cultures and what they value in their candidates.

## Moving Forward

The journey through these interviews has been both challenging and enlightening. While not every opportunity worked out, each experience has contributed to my growth as a developer and professional. The current opportunities with Doordash, ScaleAI, HarveyAI, and Figma represent new chapters in this journey.

## Conclusion

To anyone going through a similar journey, remember that every interview is a learning experience. Success isn't just about getting the job – it's about growing through the process and becoming a better developer with each attempt.

The tech industry is vast and full of opportunities. While some doors may close, others will open, often leading to even better possibilities. Stay persistent, keep learning, and most importantly, believe in your journey.
